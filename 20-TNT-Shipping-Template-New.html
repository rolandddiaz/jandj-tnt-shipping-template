Freight Details - PLEASE READ


  1. This item will be freighted with TNT.
  2. We will strictly only freight to the address you have listed on your eBay account. Please check your address carefully when proceeding through checkout.
  3. Please ensure you have a current phone number listed when proceeding through checkout.

  5. TNT will charge a $25 fee if there is no permission to leave note and they are forced to re-deliver.
  6. TNT will not deliver to Post Offices or PO Boxes.
 

Combined Postage on Multiple Purchases

If you wish to purchase more than one item and have your postage combined please follow these instructions:
 
1. Email us for a quote OR
2. Purchase the items and then click ‘REQUEST TOTAL FROM SELLER’.
 
Please note that we cannot combine postage costs if you have already paid for your items.
 
We try as best as we can to charge only what Australia Post charges us.

About Jono & Johno

 
Jono & Johno began operating in the rural town of Warracknabeal in 2008.
 
In 2011 we moved to Ballarat in the Central Highlands and have since gone on to become one of the leading online suppliers in chainsaw equipment, power machinery, spare parts and farming supplies.
 
We pride ourselves on our product knowledge and on our unbeatable customer service. Jono & Johno is proudly Australian owned and operated.
